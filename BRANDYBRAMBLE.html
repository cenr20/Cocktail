With its blend of sweet and sour flavours, the bramble is refreshing yet cosy and best of all, boozy! For those autumnal nights, a warming cocktail is the perfect drink to keep the cold at bay.

Ingredients

You will need;

  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Strainer
  • 50ml of your preffered gin (this recipe uses London Dry Gin)
  • 25ml of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 12.5ml of sugar syrup
  • 12.5ml of Creme de Mure

For garnish;

  • Crushed ice
  • Fresh Blackberries
  • Lemon
  • Old Fashioned glass

Method

  1. Fill the glass with crushed ice
  2. Pour the Gin, lemon Juice and sugar syrup into your shaker, half-fill with crushed ice, and shake until ice cold
  3. Strain the mixture into the glass over the crushed ice
  4. Drizzle the Creme de Mure over the top. It will filter through the ice an settle at the bottom
  5. Stick a blakcberry and a lemon wedge onto a cocktail stick and add to glass
  6. Drink up and enjoy
