Pina Colada

8 Reviews

A creamy, smooth Pina Colada will transport you right to a summers day on the beach.

  • Serves 1
  • 5 Minutes
  • Easy

  • Ingredients

  • Method

  • 60ml Coconut Cream
  • 120ml Pineapple juice
  • 50ml White Rum


  1. Pulse all the ingredients along with a handful of ice in a blender until smooth
  2. Pour into a tall glass with a few more crushed ice cubes
  3. Garnish with a wedge of pineapple, and an umbrella if you are feeling particularly tropical!
