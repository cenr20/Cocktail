Moscow Mule

Zesty and refreshing, this classic Moscow Mule is a real crowd pleaser. Traditionally served in a copper mug which takes on the cool temperature of the drink.

Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 50 ml Vodka
  • Juice of half a lime
  • 100 ml Ginger beer
  • Crushed ice

Panel Content

Method

  1. Fill a copper mug half full with crushed ice.
  2. Pour in the vodka, lime juice and ginger beer, and stir.
  3. Garnish with a wedge of lime and a sprig of mint
